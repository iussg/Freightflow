Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FreightFlow

Booking & Search

Booking is simple: (1) Enter pickup and delivery locations, (2) Select truck type, (3) Browse available trucks and prices, (4) Choose your preferred transporter, (5) Fill load details and make payment. Your booking confirmation will be sent instantly via SMS and email.

You can book trucks up to 30 days in advance. However, we recommend booking 2-3 days before pickup for the best availability and pricing. Last-minute bookings (same day or next day) may have limited options depending on your route.

Yes. Free cancellation is available up to 12 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 12 hours incur a 10% fee. Modifications (date, time, location) can be made up to 6 hours before pickup at no extra charge, subject to availability.

Pricing & Payments

Pricing is based on: distance (per km rate), truck type and capacity, loading/unloading charges, toll estimates, and 5% GST. Our AI optimizes pricing by considering route demand, time of booking, and transporter availability to give you the best rates.

We accept all major payment methods: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking (all major banks), and Digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay). All transactions are 256-bit SSL encrypted.

Yes, GST-compliant invoices are automatically generated after payment and available for download in your dashboard. Invoices include all required details: GSTIN, HSN code, itemized breakdown, and tax calculations. PDF format suitable for accounting.

Tracking & Delivery

Track shipments in real-time through: (1) Dashboard - click "Track" on your booking, (2) Tracking URL sent via SMS/email, (3) Mobile app notifications. You'll see live GPS location, estimated arrival time, route progress, and driver contact details.

You'll receive instant notifications if delays occur. Common reasons include traffic, weather, or checkpoints. Contact the driver directly via in-app calling/messaging, or reach our 24/7 support at 1800-XXX-XXXX. We monitor all shipments and proactively inform you of any issues.

Verification & Trust

Every transporter undergoes triple verification: (1) Document check - GST, PAN, vehicle RC, driver's license, (2) Background screening - police verification, previous employer checks, (3) Performance tracking - ratings, on-time delivery rate, complaint history. Only verified partners appear on our platform.
